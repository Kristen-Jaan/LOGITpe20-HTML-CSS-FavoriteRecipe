Ingredients

For the cookie

Ingredient Amount
Butter 10 Tablespoons
Brown Sugar 2/3 cups
White Sugar 1/3 cup
Egg + Egg Yolk 1 of each
Vanilla 1 Teaspoon
All-Purpose Flour 1 2/3 cup
Baking Soda 3/4 Teaspoon
Salt 3/4 Teaspoon
Chocolate Chip 1 Cup

For the frosting

Ingredient Amount
Butter 4 Tablespoons
Cocoa Powder 2 Tablespoons
Powdered Sugar 1 1/4 Cup
Vanilla 1/2 Teaspoon
Milk 1-2 Tablespoons
Sprinkles idk

Instructions

For the cookie

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and grease a (8 or 9 inch) pie plate or cake pan.
  2. Cream the butter and sugars together for 2 minutes and then mix in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla.
  3. Add the dry ingredients and mix until combined. Use a wooden spoon to mix in all but 2 tablespoons of chocolate chips and then press the cookie dough into the pie plate. Sprinkle the remaining 2 tablespoons chocolate chips on top and press them down slightly.
  4. Bake for 22-25 minutes. Let the cookie cool completely and then run a sharp knife along the edge to loosen and then use a large spatula (or two!) to transfer to a platter. Pipe on frosting along the edges and add the sprinkles.

For the frosting

  1. Beat all the frosting ingredients together until smooth, adding milk as needed. Place into a piping bag fitted with a 1M tip. Pipe on frosting. Sprinkle on the sprinkles.
